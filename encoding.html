Encoding

Automatic vs. Effortful Processing

Automatic Effortful
Unconscious Conscious
Incidental and well-learned information Information that requires effort and attention to learn
Ex. Spongebob's address or Squidward's middle name while watching Spongebob Ex. Learning psychology while in class

Effortful Processing Images

Chunking
- Dividng and grouping together parts of a large piece of information in order to remember it easily
- Ex. 1776149118121941 = 1776-1491-1812-1941
Mnemonics
- Memory aids such as acronyms and memorable phrases
- Ex. PEMDAS(Order of operations), SOH-CAH-TOA(Trigonometry)
Imagery
- Mentally generated images
- Ex. Imagining a lush green field with a large, brown oak tree in the middle

Spacing Effect

Spacing out learning and practice over the course of multiple days is much more effective than practice compressed into a short period of time.
