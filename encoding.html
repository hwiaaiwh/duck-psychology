Automatic vs Effortful Processing

  • Automatic: unconscious encoding
  • Effortful: conscious encoding

Explict vs Implict

  • Explicit - conscious facts and experiences
  • Implicit - unconscious knowledge

Effortful Processing Strategies

  • Chunking - grouping together parts of information to remember it easily
  • Mnemonics - acronyms, memory aids
  • Imagery - mental images

Spacing Effect

  • Defintion: spaced out practice > repeated practice for long term
  • E.g. Anki vs Quizlet

Shallow vs Deep Processing

  • Shallow - memory without meaning attached (forgetful)
