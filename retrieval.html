Misinformation Effect

  • The distortion of a past memory with an external suggestion and/or misinformation.
  • E.g. You remember that the cars were faster when it crashed because everyone else implied it was, but in reality the cars were slower and the crash was less severe.

Source Amnesia

  • Inability to recall origin of memory but retain the main information
  • E.g. Remembering a certain character's quote but not remembering who that character is or what show they came from.
